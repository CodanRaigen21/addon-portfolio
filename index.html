Buildable Vehicles

With this experiment, I developed a way to detect blocks and if all conditions are met, I can create spawn a mob and drive it. The way you control the mob is different from a horse. You need to hold down a "pedal" to actually drive and looking upwards or downwards can trigger a smoother turn, allowing you to drift.
